Deciphered Plaintext: titanic is an american epic romance and disaster film directed, written, produced, and co edited by xames cameron. incorporating both historical and fictionalized aspects, it is based on accounts of the sinking of the rms titanic, and stars leonardo dicaprio and kate winslet as members of different social classes who fall in love aboard the ship during its ill fated maiden voyage. cameron inspiration for the film came from his fascination with shipwrecks, he felt a love story interspersed with the human loss would be essential to convey the emotional impact of the disaster. production began when cameron shot footage of the actual titanic wreck. another good film.
